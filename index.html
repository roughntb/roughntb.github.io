You clicked on a phishing email. Please take a minute to review the indicators found in the email you received. This will help in analyzing whether an email you received is legitimate or phishing in nature.
SPOT FAKE EMAILS AND STAY SAFE
Fake address or web links
The sender's displayed name and email address do not match the company the email represents or the links redirect to websites not associated with the purported company.
Non-Coforge source
Email from Non Coforge sources contain a warning in the email about it. Please check that the email is received from a verified source.
High urgency or threats
The email creates a high sense of urgency, or threatens consequences for inaction.
Report Message
When you receive a phishing email, use the Report Message addin from your outlook to report it. This will help us take quick containment action and block such emails.
Requests sensitive information
The email requests you to follow a link to login, or requests sensitive information such as credit card number or password.
Contact us!
Always share any suspicious email with AllatCIC@Coforgetech.com
